# Simon Mobility - Product Overview

## Product Description

Simon is a mobile-first web application designed to centralize vehicle-related services for Colombian drivers.

The application allows users to manage their vehicle services in one place, including insurance, documentation, and mobility assistance.

The goal is to simplify the interaction between users, insurers, and mobility services through a single digital platform.

---

## Core Value Proposition

Simon provides drivers with a **single platform for vehicle services**, reducing friction and making it easier to manage essential tasks such as insurance purchases and renewals.

The product focuses on:

- simplicity
- speed
- clarity
- accessibility

Users should be able to complete important actions in **3–5 steps maximum**.

---

## Key Product Pillars

### 1. Insurance management

Users can view and manage their vehicle insurance policies.

### 2. Insurance acquisition

Users can purchase insurance policies or request contact from an advisor.

### 3. Vehicle information

Users can keep track of important vehicle information and documents.

### 4. Assistance services

Users can access mobility-related services connected to their vehicle.

---

## Long Term Vision

Simon aims to become a **digital assistant for vehicle ownership**, providing proactive services such as:

- insurance reminders
- renewal notifications
- mobility alerts
- assistance services
- vehicle management tools

The app should evolve from a transactional platform into a **daily-use mobility companion**.
