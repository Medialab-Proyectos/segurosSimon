# User Segments

Simon serves different types of vehicle owners. Understanding these segments is essential for designing effective user experiences.

---

## Segment 1: Existing Insurance Customers

Users who already have an insurance policy through Promotex or partner insurers.

### Goals

- View policy details
- Check expiration dates
- Download insurance documents
- Renew policies
- Access assistance services

### Behaviors

These users interact with the app mainly to:

- verify information
- manage their insurance
- handle claims or assistance

---

## Segment 2: New Users

Users who download the application without an existing insurance policy.

### Goals

- Explore available services
- Compare insurance plans
- Purchase insurance
- Contact advisors

### Behaviors

These users need guidance through:

- clear offers
- simple purchase flows
- educational content

---

## Segment 3: Occasional Users

Users who only open the application when they need to perform a specific task.

Examples:

- renewing insurance
- downloading policy documents
- checking vehicle information

The product must reduce friction and allow quick task completion.

---

## Key Behavioral Insight

Most users will interact with the app:

- early in the morning before work
- late at night
- when a reminder triggers an action

Therefore the app must support **fast, minimal-interaction flows**.
